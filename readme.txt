== Description ==

Monitor your online reputation and get alerted immediately when your brand (or custom keywords) are mentioned on the web, with RankScanner Buzz - the ultimate Google Alerts replacement - that automatically makes sure to keep an eye on any talk going on about you, your brand or other keywords anywhere on the internet.

= Web monitoring features =

*   Automatic alerts when mentioned on the web
*   Notification of when mentions happened
*   Detailed real-time overview in WordPress
*   Direct links to the source
*   ... and much more!

Create a free account, or use your existing RankScanner account, to get started immediately.

= Get alerts for any keyword mention on the web =

Want to know when people talk about your blog? With RankScanner Buzz you can monitor certain keywords and phrases on the web and get alerted (automatically via email) as soon as someone mentions the particular keyword online, whether it is on social media, blogs or other places on the internet.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New'
2. Search for 'RankScanner: Buzz'
3. Activate 'RankScanner: Buzz' from your plugins page

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download 'RankScanner: Buzz' plugin here or on [RankScanner's official website](https://www.rankscanner.com/wordpress/)
2. Use an FTP client to upload the 'rankscanner-buzz' folder to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, or simply go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New' > 'Upload plugin' from your WordPress dashboard and upload the .zip file there
3. Activate 'RankScanner: Buzz' from the plugins page

After activating the plugin, all you have to do is to navigate to 'RankScanner' > 'Buzz' in the WP sidebar, and you can start adding mention alerts to your domain which will then be monitored automatically several times a day.

You are now good to go! RankScanner will automatically monitor your brand or search phrases on the web and social media, and detailed discoveries will be delivered directly to your WP-admin as well as your email. Enjoy!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How up-to-date are the alerts for Google and social media? =

The keywords/alerts will be almost instant, as RankScanner Buzz constantly keeps checking for new mentions and can therefore deliver them to you quickly and without almost no delay. The email reports are generated a maximum of once per day, but this setting can be adjusted upon your needs.

= Is it possible to monitor more keywords? =

Definitely. Just refer some friends or colleagues, or simply upgrade your domain to one of the extra plans.
